Terminology

  1. Contacts: Every one of our clients is a contact. Anyone who wants us to work for them or who wants to pay us needs to be entered as a contact.
  2. Jobs: Every project we do gets its own job. Jobs can be big projects with a lot of parts (like "Wale Concert Graphics") or small jobs (like "Upstate Technology Servies Business Cards"). Try to use super-awesome and descriptive job names, that's all that will show up on the invoice.
  3. Products: Products are parts of jobs. This is stuff like "Retractable Banner with Stand" or "2x3 Mounted Sign." Products are made up of the materials you'll use for the job. Every product has to be attached to a Job (so make the Job first, before you try and create Products).
  4. Invoices: We bill our clients with invoices. These get created from Jobs that you aren't going to add any more Products to. When you take payment from a client, you can add Payments to the Invoice to track incoming revenue.
